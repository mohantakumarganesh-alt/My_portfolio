# Expanded Outline for 50‑page Research Report

1. **Executive Summary**
   - Brief overview of objectives, methodology, key findings, and policy implications.
2. **Introduction**
   - Context of agricultural mechanization.
   - Problem statement and research questions.
   - Scope and significance.
3. **Literature Review**
   - Historical perspective on mechanization.
   - Recent studies (last 5 years) on employment impacts.
   - Theoretical frameworks (e.g., dual‑labour market, technology adoption).
4. **Methodology**
   - Data sources (surveys, census, farm records).
   - Sampling strategy and variables.
   - Statistical techniques (regression, difference‑in‑differences).
5. **Results**
   - Descriptive statistics.
   - Impact on employment by region and farm size.
   - Tables and figures (e.g., employment trends, mechanization index).
6. **Discussion**
   - Interpretation of results.
   - Comparison with literature.
   - Implications for rural livelihoods.
7. **Policy Recommendations**
   - Strategies for mitigating job loss.
   - Support for skill development and mechanization adoption.
8. **Future Work**
   - Suggested extensions (e.g., longitudinal studies, other crops).
9. **Conclusion**
   - Summary of findings and final remarks.
10. **References**
    - Expanded bibliography with recent sources.
11. **Appendices**
    - Raw data tables, questionnaire, additional figures.
